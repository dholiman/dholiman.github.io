The Challenge:

Transforming a Legacy App for Global Reach

Our main challenge lay in seamlessly integrating a new brand identity into the app while opening access to the world. This endeavor required not only visual redesign but also the implementation of language support, currency exchange rates, and compliance with various legal requirements across regions.

The Goal:

Empowering Worldwide Users with Accurate Data

Our paramount goal was to expand app accessibility across diverse regions, languages, and legal frameworks while enhancing the accuracy of earnings projections. Simultaneously, we strove to maintain trust during a rebranding process, ensuring a seamless transition for existing users.

The Process:

Global Asperations in a Lean Environment

Despite resource constraints, our lean team – comprising myself, two developers, and a project manager – collaborated closely to streamline research, design, and development processes.

After multiple iterations involving wireframes, mockups, and user tests, we presented our prototype for the international release. The results were promising, with a significant expected increase in user engagement.

The Next Steps:

Leveraging Insights for Continuous Enhancement

Following the successful launch, our focus shifted to analyzing user feedback and behavior, directing efforts towards bug fixes, optimization, and feature augmentation. With an ongoing commitment to understanding user behaviors, we aimed to use new insights on user behavior guide future enhancements and sustain success.

The Conclusion:

Accurate, Worldwide Predictions

The evolution of YL Insights into a globally accessible and trusted tool underscores the impact of strategic design and collaboration. By prioritizing user feedback and employing lean methodologies, we adeptly navigated the challenges of rebranding and globalization, empowering users worldwide to achieve their business objectives.

Primary Achievement:

385.97% Increase in User Engagement

This project exemplifies how human-centric design decisions can significantly influence company KPIs, particularly in delivering dynamic data tables on mobile devices.

Bonus Achievement:

Laying the Foundation for Innovation

Understanding the unique needs of our global user base has positioned us to tailor strategies effectively, ensuring sustained growth and success for YL Insights.
