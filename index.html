A little bit about me.

As a product designer with a mechanical engineering background and a BFA in graphic design, I thrive on collaborative design strategies. I dive deep into problem-solving, leverage user research for impactful changes, and streamline workflows. While my knack for adaptability ensures I stay on the pulse of design systems and trends, my mission will always be to use design to advocate for the people using my product.

When not head down in a high-fidelity prototype, you’ll find me drinking copious amounts of coffee, admiring the work of my favorite artists, and tinkering with things I have no business tinkering with.

And yes, bad leading does keep me up at night.
