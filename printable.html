Fellow Stagg: Rise and Hide

After buying a broken Ender 3 from a thrift and repairing it, I began my dive into 3D printing. My first print (apart from Benchy of course) was a kettle riser. I found the file online for free, printed it out, and added some rubber feet to stop some rattles when using it.

Fast forward a few months. Somewhere shared their version in r/pourover, and this one had a drawer to help clean it up. Many people were ready to buy, but some wanted the files to print themselves. While this redditor decided to only sell physical prints, it sparked the idea in me to share my own version.

This is that version. Updated to fit most common scales, adding a drawer for a tidy look, and ensuring the drawer face does not interfere with the use of the scale during brewing.
