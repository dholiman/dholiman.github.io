TLDR:

Define, Design, Validate, Iterate.

My design process is adaptive and collaborative, allowing it to be shaped by stakeholders, colleagues, and users. Typically, I begin with in-depth research to understand the problem. I then move into ideation, where creativity and strategy converge to create meaningful concepts.

High-fidelity designs are crafted with a focus on accessibility and user experience. Finally, the designs are validated through testing, refined, and collaboratively implemented, ensuring the final product is polished and effective.

Define:

Understanding the Problem is the Foundation of Success

I begin with comprehensive research, including user interviews, surveys, and data analysis, to deeply understand the project's needs. By engaging with stakeholders and analyzing competitors, I ensure my designs are well-informed and strategically aligned with business goals.

Design:

Where possibilities meet reality

With a clear understanding of the problem, I transition into the design phase where creativity and strategy merge. Through brainstorming, sketching, and prototyping, I explore multiple ideas and refine them into high-fidelity designs. I pay close attention to visual hierarchy, color theory, and accessibility, ensuring the final product is both functional and inclusive.

Validate:

Ensuring Effectiveness

Validation is key to refining the design. I conduct usability tests, A/B testing, and gather feedback from real users to ensure the design meets user needs and business objectives. This data-driven approach helps me fine-tune the design for optimal performance.

Iterate:

Success or Failure, Learn from Both

The design process doesn’t end at launch. I believe in continuous improvement and learning. Post-launch analysis and ongoing feedback allow me to refine and enhance the design over time, ensuring it remains effective and aligned with evolving user and business needs.

Conclusion:

A Dynamic, User-Centered Approach

My design process is dynamic and user-centered, evolving with each project to ensure the solutions I create are both effective and aligned with user needs and business goals.

By starting with a well-defined process, I can adapt to the unique demands of each project, accelerating the journey and delivering an exceptional final product.

A little bit about me.

As a product designer with a mechanical engineering background and a BFA in graphic design, I thrive on collaborative design strategies. I dive deep into problem-solving, leverage user research for impactful changes, and streamline workflows. While my knack for adaptability ensures I stay on the pulse of design systems and trends, my mission will always be to use design to advocate for the people using my product.

When not head down in a high-fidelity prototype, you’ll find me drinking copious amounts of coffee, admiring the work of my favorite artists, and tinkering with things I have no business tinkering with.
